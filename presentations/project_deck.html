Culture

The culture: let's grow our family

We should be a friend group, not a club -> we work hard AND play hard

Everyday vibe

  • Coffee chats outside your project
  • Low-pressure group chats / memes / rants
  • People checking in “how are you” not “is the deck done”
  • Events that aren’t forceful, or like club planned, but rather something that is more natural

Intentional stuff

  • Delta “families” / pods mixing all years
  • Cross-project dinners & game nights
  • Case advisor activities
  • Maintain traditions but also growing

Wellbeing

We can all push ourselves without burning out

We all care about doing good work. But we’re also students, people with lives, and sometimes barely sleeping.

  • Reasonability not heroics
  • Norms around hard work
  • Making it normal to say "I'm drowning this week"
  • Checking in on people, not just timelines

Goal

  • Delta = place you grow, not break
  • Projects you’re proud of, not resent
  • People who still want to hang after the project ends
